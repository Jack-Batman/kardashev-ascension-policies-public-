Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved locally. For cloud backup, sign in with Google or Apple in Settings to sync across devices.

I lost my progress. Can it be recovered?

If you had cloud save enabled (Google/Apple sign-in), simply sign in on your new device to restore. If you only had local saves, unfortunately progress cannot be recovered after an uninstall.

How do offline earnings work?

Your civilization continues producing energy while you're away, up to a maximum offline duration. Return to the game to collect your accumulated earnings. Cosmic Members enjoy extended offline hours.

What is Dark Matter used for?

Dark Matter is a premium currency earned through prestige resets, daily rewards, challenges, and optional purchases. Use it to buy permanent upgrades, unlock Architects, and accelerate research.

How does the Prestige system work?

Once you reach certain Kardashev milestones, you can Prestige to reset your energy and generators in exchange for Dark Matter and permanent production bonuses. Each prestige makes your next run faster.

I made a purchase but didn't receive my items.

First, try restarting the app. If items still haven't appeared, go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." If the issue persists, contact us at the email above with your purchase receipt.

How do I cancel my Cosmic Membership subscription?

iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Kardashev Ascension > Cancel
Android: Google Play Store > Subscriptions > Kardashev Ascension > Cancel

The game is running slowly or crashing.

Try closing other apps, restarting your device, or reinstalling the game (make sure cloud save is enabled first). Kardashev Ascension works best on devices running iOS 14+ or Android 8+.

About the Game

Kardashev Ascension is an idle civilization game based on the Kardashev Scale — the scientific framework for measuring a civilization's technological advancement by its energy consumption.


Ascend through 5 cosmic eras:

  • Era I Planetary — Harness your homeworld's energy
  • Era II Stellar — Build Dyson Spheres around your star
  • Era III Galactic — Harvest black holes and quasars
  • Era IV Universal — Manipulate spacetime and entropy
  • Era V Multiversal — Transcend reality itself
